PayPal setup

  1. Create or log into your PayPal business account.
  2. Create a PayPal REST app and copy the live client ID.
  3. Open config.js and replace YOUR_PAYPAL_CLIENT_ID.
  4. Deploy the site to your domain and make a small live test order.

For production, a small backend is recommended for secure order storage, email notifications and PayPal webhooks.
